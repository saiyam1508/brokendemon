ABOUT ARTIST:

R.CHARAN

Charan Ravi, professionally known as Broken Demon is a 20 year old music producer from India.
Coming from a noisy city like Bangalore, his sound really stands out of the crowd and cuts through the noise.
Charan mainly produces Trap, Hybrid Trap and experiments with almost every form of sound.
Being heavily inspired by Skrillex, Eptic & Troyboi, he adds his own flavours to the table.
